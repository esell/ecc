About

The Code Creator page is a simple site that allows you to create basic substitution ciphers and then encode/decode text based on the created cipher. The idea came from watching my child exchange paper ciphers and messages with is friends at school. I thought it would be a neat project to turn into something web-based, so here we are!

Please note that this is not intended to be a secure service and is for educational purposes only. It was designed with elementary school aged children in mind, and is meant to stir up interest in cryptography, nothing else :).

How does it work?

A cipher page is your own spot to manage your cipher and encode/decode text based on the cipher you have created. So how do you create a cipher page?

  1. First, come up with a word, phrase, etc that you can remember. This is even more important if you plan on sharing your cipher page with others.
  2. Second, check and see if it is available. You can do this by adding it onto the end of https://ethanscodcreator.com/. As an example, pretend you came up with the phrase dumdum, you would vist https://ethanscodecreator.com/dumdum. If your page is not available, you will see a red bar at the top of the page letting you know that someone else has already claimed this. No problem, just come up with a new word/phrase and try it again! Once you've found an un-claimed page, move on to the next step
  3. Third, now that you've found an un-claimed page, claim it! You do this by entering a secret into the secret box and hitting the save button on the page. Now anytime you want to update your cipher, you'll need to provide this secret.
  4. Fourth, share your cipher page with others so that you can encode and decode messages based on the cipher you've created on your cipher page!
